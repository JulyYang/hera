About HERA

HERA is an experimental decision-support tool designed to assist local and regional organizations in planning and preparedness for hazardous and extreme meteorological and climatological events. The tool provides decision support through:

  • Hazard data visualization in county maps and annual tables
  • County-level counts and probabilities of hazardous and extreme events
  • Comparisons across counties
Currently, the tool uses historical data to provide information by county in North Carolina and South Carolina, and may soon be expanded to other southeastern states. The original version of HERA can be found here.

HERA is a CISA-SERCC collaboration and a team effort led by Ferdouz Cochran and Chip Konrad. Other team members include Montana Eck, Amanda Farris, Yates McConnell, Sandra Rayne, William Schmitz, Hunter Spitzer, and July Yang.
