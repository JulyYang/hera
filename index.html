About HERA

HERA is an experimental decision-support tool designed to assist local and regional organizations in planning and preparedness for hazardous and extreme meteorological and climatological events. The tool provides decision support through: Hazard data visualization in county maps and annual tables County-level counts and probabilities of hazardous and extreme events Comparisons across counties Currently, the tool uses historical data to provide information by county in North Carolina and South Carolina, and may soon be expanded to other southeastern states.

Settings

To navigate through HERA, click on the Hazard tabs or move the navigation slider that appears under tabs when hovering over them. On each page, you may select State, Years, and Subcategories depending on the hazard of interest.
